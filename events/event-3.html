Kolkata street food — reimagined, refined, deeply personal · Gurgaon, NCR

About This Evening

There is nothing quite like the first rains in Kolkata. The city slows, the streets steam, and the smell of petrichor mingles with the smoke from roadside stalls frying telebhaja and ghugni for the evening crowd.

Monsoon Mela is our tribute to that sensory memory — Kolkata street food taken apart, examined, and put back together with the same care and intention we bring to every Toontooni's Table dinner.

This is comfort food elevated: familiar enough to feel like home, surprising enough to spark conversation. A dinner for rainy evenings and full hearts.

The Menu

  1. Street Snacks — Telebhaja flight: begun, alur chop, peyaji
  2. First — Ghugni bruschetta, tamarind gel, raw onion
  3. Second — Kati roll deconstructed, egg, kasundi mayo
  4. Main — Kosha mangsho, lachha paratha, pickled salad
  5. Dessert — Jilipi with rabri, orange zest
