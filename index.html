The Experience

Toontooni's
Table

Toontooni's Table is an intimate dining experience serving modern Bengali food. Our purpose is to create a warm atmosphere of community, good food, interesting conversations, and new friendships.

We've hosted 28 events and served 400+ guests (and counting) so far, while also partnering with 15+ brands to create curated culinary experiences.

Bengali Flavours

Here, we blend traditional Bengali flavours with modern culinary techniques to curate a menu that will blow your mind away. Seasonal produce, familiar flavours, and contemporary cooking styles guide each dish.
